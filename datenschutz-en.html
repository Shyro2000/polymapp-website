About PolymApp

PolymApp is the comprehensive app for technical professionals that makes your work easier. It offers features like tolerance calculators, thread calculations, and analysis of technical drawings. All provided data has been created as accurately as possible; however, no guarantee can be given for its accuracy.

General Information

Protecting your personal data is very important to me. This privacy policy explains what data is collected from you, how it is used, and what rights you have regarding your data.

Responsible Party

The responsible party for data processing is:
PolymApp
info@polymapp.ch

Data Collection and Processing

Using the PolymApp app does not require you to provide personal data. During the trial period, no personal data is collected or stored.

Subscription and Payment Information

If you choose a paid subscription after the trial period, certain data will be collected to manage the payment and subscription. This includes:

  • Name
  • Email address

This data is used exclusively for managing the subscription and is protected in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

Purpose of Data Processing

The data you provide is used for the following purposes:

  • Providing and improving the PolymApp app
  • Managing and processing subscriptions
  • Communicating with you regarding the use of the app and your subscription

User Account

The app allows you to create a user account. Only the email address you provide is stored for login purposes. Additionally, any settings and customizations you make within the app are linked to your user account and stored.
This data is used solely to secure and restore your individual settings within the app. No data is shared with third parties.
You can delete your user account at any time directly in the app. In that case, all stored data will be permanently removed.

Photos and Uploaded Documents

Certain features of the app allow you to take photos, upload images, or upload documents. These files are not stored on the smartphone and are only available while the relevant app feature is open.
When leaving the feature, the files are automatically deleted from the history. Neither photos nor uploaded documents are transmitted to PolymApp or third parties.

Data Sharing with Third Parties

Your data will not be shared with third parties without your explicit consent, unless required to fulfill contractual obligations or legal requirements.

Data Retention

I store your data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes mentioned above or as required by legal retention periods.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data stored about you
  • Request correction of incorrect data
  • Request deletion of your data, provided there are no legal retention obligations
  • Request restriction of the processing of your data
  • Object to the processing of your data
  • Receive your data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format (data portability)
  • File a complaint with a supervisory authority

Contact

If you have questions about the processing of your data, this privacy policy, or feedback regarding the app, you can contact me at any time via the contact form or at the following email address:
info@polymapp.ch

Changes to this Privacy Policy

I reserve the right to modify this privacy policy as needed to comply with legal requirements or to reflect changes in the app. The current privacy policy can always be found in the app.
