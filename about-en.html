My name is Fabian Schatzmann. I work as a team leader in CNC turning and have been active in machining and manufacturing for many years.

In daily work, I repeatedly noticed that essential information such as tolerances, threads, cutting data, or material values is spread across many different reference books, tables, and software tools. This costs time and increases the risk of errors.

For this reason, I developed PolymApp - a practical app created by a practitioner for practitioners. The goal was to bundle all relevant information and tools for CNC professionals in one clear, central place, also available offline.

PolymApp is designed for polymechanics, CNC machinists, setup technicians, programmers, as well as team and production managers who want to work quickly, reliably, and efficiently.

The app is continuously developed further, with a strong focus on practical value, clarity, and real-world requirements from manufacturing.
